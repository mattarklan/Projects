Core Mechatronics Projects

ROS2 Autonomous Robot

Took a differential drive robot and made it fully autonomous using a Raspberry Pi 5 and ROS2. Set up sensors, navigation, and control so it could operate without manual input.

Warman 2023 Competition (Winning Team)

Led programming and electrical design in a three-person team that won Warman 2023. Built and integrated control systems under competition constraints.

Sony Camera PCB Reverse Engineering

Recreated an obsolete Sony camera PCB by building a full schematic and new layout. Board was manufactured and worked as intended.

3 kW TPFC + PSFB EV Charger (Design Stage)

Designed a 3 kW two-phase PFC and phase-shifted full-bridge charger in KiCad. Completed schematic and PCB layout; hardware not yet built.

ESP32 3-Phase Water Heater Power Control

Used an ESP32 and 3-phase solid state relay to PWM a 13 kW resistive load. Added Wi-Fi interface for adjustable power control.

Electric Vehicle Conversion

Converted a petrol vehicle to fully electric. Mounted batteries, completed high-voltage wiring, and integrated systems.

Autonomous Tracked Lawnmower (Prototype)

Developing a tracked autonomous mower platform. One drive side complete; planned LoRa and RTK integration for navigation.

Low-Pressure Irrigation Timer
